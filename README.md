# App-store pitch

StayWake is the alarm that doesn't just wake you up — it keeps you up. Most alarm
apps (and gimmicks like "dance to dismiss") stop the second you silence them, so you
crawl right back into bed. StayWake makes you prove you're awake with a quick mission
(math, typing, shake, steps — your pick), then checks in again minutes later so you
can't fall back asleep. Win the morning, build a streak, climb your Wake Score. No
camera, works offline, ready in two taps, and actually fun. Wake up. Stay up.
